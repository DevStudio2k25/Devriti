11. AI CHAT – HELPFUL FEATURE SUGGESTIONS + PAGE REDIRECT BUTTONS

• When AI is unable to generate a response, chat shows helpful feature suggestions from inside the app
• Suggestions include: Daily Routine, Self-Care (Breathing / Sounds), Emergency Support, AI Mood Challenges, Doctor Connect (if available)
• Each suggestion appears inside the chat as an interactive button
• Tapping the button instantly redirects the user to the respective app page
• Suggestions are chosen smartly based on:
  - User's mood
  - Recent chat context
  - Emotional intensity
• Supportive, comforting language used when suggesting actions (e.g., "Take a short break?", "Try breathing for a moment.")
• Chat history remains intact after returning from the redirected page
• Ensures emotional safety and strong user support when AI cannot reply
